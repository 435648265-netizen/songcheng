Professional Bathroom Hardware Manufacturer Since 2007

Wenzhou Songcheng Trading Co., Ltd. is a specialized manufacturer of kitchen faucets, sink accessories, shower systems, and basin faucets. Our products serve the U.S. market with 1% nationwide coverage and growing. We are certified with cUPC, ISO 9001, and CE, ensuring full compliance with North American and global standards.
