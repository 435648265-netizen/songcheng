Auto Sensor Manufacturer Since 2015

Songcheng Motor is a specialized manufacturer of automotive oxygen sensors and electronic control sensors. Operating from a modern production facility in Wenzhou, Zhejiang — China's largest auto parts manufacturing cluster — we combine advanced production equipment with rigorous quality control to deliver sensors that meet or exceed OE specifications.

Our production lines are equipped with automated laser welding, ceramic element sintering, and computerized testing stations. Every sensor undergoes resistance, response time, and thermal cycling tests before leaving the factory.

We serve distributors, wholesalers, and OEM service providers across Europe, North America, Middle East, and Southeast Asia. Our engineering team can develop custom sensor solutions based on your sample or spec sheet.
